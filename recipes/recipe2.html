  • Dry Mango powder
  • chilly flakes
  • lemons/lime
  • cucumber

Preparation

Here are the key method to make this dish.

  1. 1 cup of cooked Chickpeas or you can use canned one.
  3. Chop tomatoes and onioninto small diced shape.
  4. Mashed the Avacado or you can diced them like onion and tomatoes and add them togther.
  5. Add finely chopped coriander.
  6. Then season with 1 tsp of balck pepper,1 tsp of dry Mango powder,salt and chilly as according to your taste.
  7. At the end add a half of lemon or lime into the salad and mix it properly.
