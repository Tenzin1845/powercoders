Chicken Chowmein

Chowmien has a various name country to country or way of chooking style is different too.

My Recipe is Originated from Mainland China and with simple ingridents.

Ingridents

  • Chicken
  • Bell pepper
  • onions/schallots
  • tomato
  • szechuan pepper
  • salt
  • coriander
  • baby corns
  • chilly flakes
  • lemons/lime
  • soysauce

Preparation

Here are the key method to make this dish.

  1. cut the chicken into small pieces
  3. sliced tomatoes,bell pepper and onion into thin layers.
  4. Boil the noodles for 5 mins in hot water and strain it untill fry them togther with vegetable .
  5. Add finely chopped coriander.
  6. take wonk or any fry pan, non stick would be better.
  7. Add a 2 tsp of vegetable oil and wait it until its get hot.
  8. Add onion and chicken in wonk and saute it untill its turn golden and caramel.
  9. Then add bell pepper and baby corn and mix togther
  10. Add noodles

  11. Then season with 1 tsp of sezchuan pepper,1 tsp of soy suace,salt and chilly as according to your taste and fry it well.
  12. At the end add a half of lemon or lime into the Chowmien and garnished with coriander.

Cooking Time

00:10 noodles cooking time is 00:05

How to serve

You can serve this in bowl or in plate.

Serve it with glass of hot bone broth
